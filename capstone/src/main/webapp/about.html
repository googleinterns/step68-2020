Our goals:

Hello, and thank you for visiting ECS! ECS, or Elementary Computer Science, is a website we created with two very important goals in mind.

The first is to teach the basics of Computer Science to you, no matter how young you are. Many people have never even heard about computer science until college , and when do they get to learn about it they are behind everyone else. We at ECS believe that is unfair.

Our second goal is to make this website easy to understand and learn from. We want to motivate as many people with a computer science interest as we can. Any child, no matter where they come from, should have an equal chance to learn and practice computer science!

Achieving them:

We designed this website to be completely free and designed for everyone. In the creation of this site, we always remembered our two goals.

To reach our first goal we created content-specific lesson plans. These plans will break down the basics of computer science piece by piece in a simple way. Through our easy to follow instructions and our different methods of practicing what you just learned, we will be able to make computer science understandable to children! So, our website is an easy place for everyone to learn the fundamentals of computer science.

To reach our second goal we created a lesson plan in English, but by using computer science we were able to create lesson plans in other languages as well. This means that children from all over the world and children with English as their second language will not only be able to use the site, but also ask for help from their parents when they don't understand a concept. Although their parents may not know computer science, we designed this webste to be easily understandable by all!

Who are we?

We are just three college students

This entire website, from scratch to finish, was constructed by three college students still earning their degree. We all interned for Google and were designated a team.

We created this specific website because we want everyone to succeed. We want to help the less fortunate who may not have a computer science program at their school, or those who do but struggle due to a language barrier. We believe anyone with an interest deserves the right to fight for it.

For more specific information on all of us, scroll down just a little further!
