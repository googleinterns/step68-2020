Our goals:

Hello, and thank you for visiting ECS! ECS, or Elementary Computer Science, is a website designed to achieve two very important goals. The first is to teach the fundamentals of Computer Science to kids at an early age. Many kids are not introduced to computer science until college, at which point those who have never been introduced to these topics are significantly behind those who have. We at ECS believe that is unfair. For that reason, our second goal is to make this learning environment interactive and inclusive for all kids. We want to motivate as many people with a computer science interest as we can. Any child, regardless of socioeconomic background, should have an equal opportunity to get involved with the computer science industy.

Achieving them:

We designed this website to be completely free and designed for all users. To achieve our second goal we created a lesson plan in English, but using translation programs we were able to create lesson plans in other languages as well. This means that children from other countries and children with English as their second language will not only be able to use the site, but also ask for help from their parents when they don't understand a concept. Although their parents may not know computer science, we designed this webste to be easily understandable by all. Through content-specific lesson plans, we will achieve our first goal by teaching the fundamentals of computer science in a simplified way comprehendable by children. We created easy to follow descriptions in each lesson, along with different methods of practicing what you just learned! Our website is an easy to learn destination for the fundamentals of Computer Science.
